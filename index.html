HIOA has new demands!

HIOA now demands a "oblig" finished whitinn 10 septemper!

How can they do this to us? Do they not know that we have a oblig the 15 setemper and 19 setemper? Inn the same period that Javazone beginns. A conference where over 2000 developers meet every year in Oslo spektrum. We have talked to our most trused expert, Håkon Sørby. And he has told us that he signed up for Javazone already inn June. Javazone is a place where few student have a chance to meet the real world developers.

Javazone beginns the 9 setemper and ends the 11 setemper, however students that has signed up as volunteer has to meet up the 9 septemer. This meens that the studentes on HIOA only have few days on complete the obligs in the Java. after most students have to work in the weekend to pay loans.

- written by Håkon Sørby
